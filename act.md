The Right-to-Trial and FDA Upgrade Act is the legislative framing for turning the dFDA concept into public infrastructure, patient access rights, and deployable regulatory machinery. The maintained Act-level doctrine and current legislative text now live on the War on Disease manual.

This wiki keeps a short bridge page because several local implementation documents still depend on the Act as a design input. If you want to revise the legislation, rationale, or high-level public case, update the manual. If you want the technical and regulatory work products that would support implementation, use the local docs below.
