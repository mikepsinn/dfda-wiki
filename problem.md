The core problem is that disease imposes enormous human and economic costs while the current clinical research system remains slow, expensive, exclusionary, and poorly aligned with patient needs. The maintained problem statements and supporting arguments now live on the War on Disease manual.

This wiki still references the problem because the technical and regulatory documents here are responses to that failure mode. If you want to update the narrative, burden estimates, or high-level case for change, update the manual instead of expanding this page.
