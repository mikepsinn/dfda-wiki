# Open Food Facts

Open Food Facts gathers information and data on 1,795,493 products from around the world.

Open Food Facts is a collaborative project built by tens of thousands of volunteers and managed by a non-profit organization with 3 employees.

[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/)
