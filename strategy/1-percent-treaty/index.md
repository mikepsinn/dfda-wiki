The 1% Treaty is the funding and coordination proposal for redirecting a small share of military spending into cure discovery, patient access, and shared medical research infrastructure. The maintained treaty narrative, DIH overview, and supporting feasibility arguments now live on the War on Disease manual.

This wiki keeps a short bridge page because several local technical and policy docs still assume the treaty and DIH as upstream context. If you want to update the case for the treaty, the funding story, or DIH doctrine, update the manual instead of restoring long-form content here.
