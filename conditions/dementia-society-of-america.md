# Dementia Society of America

[https://www.dementiasociety.org](https://www.dementiasociety.org/about)

The Dementia Society of America brings much-needed education, local resources, and life-enrichment to individuals and families impacted by Dementia.

We recognize dementia caregivers and innovators and raise hope by spotlighting relevant research to discover cures, causes, encourage early detection and meaningful interventions.
