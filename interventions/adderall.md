### During

Sugary snacks. Aim to eat one every ~30 minutes. (Helps maintain blood glucose levels - you'll feel less tired and happier)

Seriously - EAT AND DRINK!

Optionally: Dose one of each magnesium, R-ALA and ALCAR during.
